A Business Built On Referrals

Why Hire Us?

Full House Renovations is the leading expert in home renovations and general contracting, and has been in business since 1989 in Edmonton. We specialize in many services including ensuites, basements, kitchens, and complete home remodeling. All of our tradespeople are full time team members which enables us to hold our quality of workmanship to the highest standards. With our custom millwork shop, we are able to design, handcraft and finish your cabinetry in our own facility.

Quality

We understand that your home is a reflection of your personal style and well-being. We work with local suppliers to ensure that only quality materials are used so that your project will last for years to come.

Experience

With over a thousand satisfied customers, you can be confident in our knowledge and expertise. Our process includes a feasibility study, comprehensive design, and detailed scope of work to provide a seamless renovation experience.

Reliability

At Full House Renovations we dedicate ourselves to providing a professional relationship where trust, transparency, and reliability are key.
