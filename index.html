A Business Built On Referrals

Why Hire Us?

Full House Renovations is one of Edmonton's leading home renovation companies. Our services range in scale from window and door installation to complete home renovations.

Quality

We are unique in that all our trades are salaried employees. For the customer, this translates into higher quality work that is guaranteed by a single company.

Experience

Full House Renovations has been in business for over 10 years, and has hundreds of satisfied customers.

Reliability

All the manufacturing for renovations, such as cabinetry, is performed in-house. This allows Full House Renovations to use cost-effective, high-quality custom work for each renovation.

A Trusted Choice for Over 20 Years

We’ve been accredited with the Better Business Bureau since 1994, and our team of over 50 dedicated employees are all bonded with Lloyd Sad Insurance.

We Do It All

From Bedrooms and Bathrooms, to Basements and Bonus Rooms

We strive for perfection no matter which part of your home needs renovating. Our team of designers, journeymen, receptionists, and accountants will work with you to make your dream renovation a reality - on time, within budget and with the best craftmanship. We take great pride in what we do, and nothing makes us happier than a job well done and a satisfied client. Since we’ve started, we’ve completed over 5,000 renovations - that’s a lot of happy customers!

Drop us a line today, and let's chat about your exciting project.
