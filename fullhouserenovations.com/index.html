Full House and the BBB
Full House Renovations has been a member of the Better Business Bureau since 1994.


 Full House Renovations is one of Edmonton's leading home renovation companies. Our services range in scale from window and door installation to complete home renovations.

Full House Renovations offers several compelling advantages over other renovation companies:
  • Full House Renovations has been in business for over 10 years, and has hundreds of satisfied customers.
  • We are unique in that all our trades are salaried employees. For the customer, this translates into higher quality work that is guaranteed by a single company.
  • All the manufacturing for renovations, such as cabinetry, is performed in-house. This allows Full House Renovations to use cost-effective, high-quality custom work for each renovation.
