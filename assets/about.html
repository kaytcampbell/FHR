About

What Makes Us Special

Full House Renovations has been in business for over 27 years (since 1989), with over 1,000 satisfied customers. We are unique in that all our trades are salaried employees. For the customer, this translates into higher quality work that is guaranteed by a single company.

Full House Renovations has its own custom millwork shop which enables us to manufacture all our own cabinetry and millwork for our renovation jobs. We also have our own custom cabinet shop with 5 cabinet makers, manufacturing all our own custom cabinetry and millwork for all our renovation jobs.

We have won multiple SAM awards, been featured on television and in multiple magazines.
